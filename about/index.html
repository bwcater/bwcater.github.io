About

I am Billy Wayne Cater. My friends call me Wayne!

I am a songwriter, but isn’t everyone in Nashville? I play guitar and tinker with other instruments – piano / keyboard, and bass guitar. I am an ASCAP member. You can find some of my music work on Apple Music, Amazon Music, Spotify, and others.

I’ve got a day job too! I’ve been a software developer for 25+ years. Over the past several years I have spent my time using C#.Net, SQL Server, ASP .Net Core (WebAPI), and Angular. More recently I have become focused on learning all things Elixir.

Around 2013, I started sketching and drawing caricatures of coworkers. One thing leads to another and I found that I enjoyed sketching landscapes, and painting watercolors. Checkout some of my art on Instagram.

In April 2021, my first children’s book was published – Looking For Friends In My Backyard. I wrote and illustrated it for my grandsons, but I am happy to share it with you and your little ones. You can purchase it on Amazon, Barnes & Noble, Books-A-Millon, and Walmart.

—Wayne
