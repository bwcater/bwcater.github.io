    Long Way from Home

    July 2017 Album

    Containing 12 original songs and recorded in 2017, Long Way from Home features a mix of songs ranging from warm ballads like Don’t Go Away to upbeat Goin' Down This Road. Thought provoking lyrical tracks like Things to Think About remind one of what is important at the end of the day! There is an underlying feel to much of this recording that is Eagle’s like.
