ABOUT

I am Billy Wayne Cater. My friends call me Wayne!

I’ve been a software developer for 25 years. That’s my day job, and one of my creative outlets. I work for Pilgrim Consulting in Franklin, Tennessee. Some of the current technologies I use are C#.Net, SQL Server, the ASP .Net Core (MVC / API), and occasionally Swift.

I am also a songwriter, but isn’t everyone in Nashville? Guitar is my main instrument while I tinker with piano / keyboard, and bass guitar. I am an ASCAP member too! You can find some of my music work on Apple Music, Amazon Music, Google Play Music, Spotify, and others.

Around 2013, I started sketching and drawing caricatures of coworkers. One thing leads to another and I found that I enjoyed sketching landscapes, and painting watercolors. Over the past few years I have painted numbers of them learning techniques by studying the works of others online, reading numbers of books, and spending time behind the brush forming my own style. Friends, coworkers, and some folks online became interested in my work, so I started sharing them on cards, postcards, and as gifts. I have done some commission work, but I am selective about that type of thing. I mainly produce what I enjoy doing and I occasionally sell that.

You can view some of my cartoon / caricature work on Instagram. If you see a piece that you are interested in contact me regarding availability and price.

--Wayne
