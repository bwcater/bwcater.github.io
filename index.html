Another side...

In 2006, my wife and I moved our family and business, South Trace Media Company, to the Nashville, Tennessee area. South Trace Media's focus was largely about photography, but since we had a broad mix of needed skills we branched out into additional multimedia work like creating posters and album covers, building web sites, recording and mixing radio ads, and creating / editing electronic press kits for other musicians.

After a while I started doing web design and development for businesses in the Nashville area, but the photography and artistic work was always on my mind. Occasionally I shot weddings, family portraits, pics of products for promotional materials, architectural shots for business websites, and other subjects.

Needing an outlet of artful expression, I began sketching and painting (watercolor) in my personal time. Once in a while I agree to do commission work.

You can view some of my art and photography on Instagram. If you see a piece that you are interested in contact me regarding availability and price.
