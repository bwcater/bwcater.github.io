About

I am Billy Wayne Cater. My friends call me Wayne!

I am a songwriter, but isn’t everyone in Nashville? Guitar is my main instrument while I tinker with piano / keyboard, and bass guitar. I am an ASCAP member. You can find some of my music work on Apple Music, Amazon Music, Spotify, and others.

I've got a day job too! I’ve been a software developer for 25 years. It's one of my creative outlets. The technologies I use these days are C#.Net, SQL Server, ASP .Net Core (API), and Angular.

Around 2013, I started sketching and drawing caricatures of coworkers. One thing leads to another and I found that I enjoyed sketching landscapes, and painting watercolors. Over the past few years I have painted numbers of them learning techniques by studying the works of others online, reading numbers of books, and spending time behind the brush forming my own style. Friends, coworkers, and some folks online became interested in my work, so I started sharing them on cards, postcards, and as gifts. I have done some commission work, but I am selective about that type of thing. I mainly focus on what I enjoy doing and I occasionally sell them.

--Wayne
